Objective

Brief intro to our organization

The objective of this program is to identify business enthusiasts or entrepreneurs and help them to grow their enterprise. We will make them realize the social aspect of the program and develop a channel through which they can give back to society.

Helping entrepreneurs set up their own enterprise, to help raise the standard of living for themselves and their families.
Generate employment for the community through the enterprise set up.
Instil a sense of gratitude and social responsibility in everyone associated with the program.

vision

Brief intro to our organization

We aim to build a chain of socially motivated enterprises, giving them resources and strategies to develop their business and generate employment to ensure the economic development of educated unemployed youth. We aim to nurture socially motivated and dedicated entrepreneurs by providing them workforce, resources, and strategies to set up and expand their own enterprise and generate employment.

Help an existing business: Under this section, we will collaborate with the existing local businesses and help them to gain a bigger audience and markets. We will provide them
Help individuals with startup ideas to set up a fresh startup or business - We can help the entrepreneur by providing some initial funds and other resources to develop the product. We can also find some programs which help such innovative business ideas.
Develop a business model and find interested people to take it forward - We can also work on some business model and help it to gain a significant structure. For this we can hire interns or other skilled individuals who are ready to work on the idea. Although, during the process or before the registration of the company we shall find someone who will be taking over the business as his own.

procedure

Brief intro to our organization

1

Any individual or a company willing to register under PRAYAS needs to submit a business proposal in a predefined format.

2

After receiving the proposal, it shall be reviewed by the PRAYAS team. The review process comprises judging the feasibility of the proposal and developing a strategy to be implemented.

3

After the plan of action has been developed, our executive team shall take care of the execution of the action plan.

target audience

Brief intro to our organization

Moral

The individual must be honest and should neither be encouraging of any form of malpractices nor be involved in any form of substance abuse (Alcohol, weed). The business idea must be constructive and useful to the community.

Dedicated

The individual must be willing to take complete responsibility of the enterprise and must put necessary and consistent efforts in order to ensure that the enterprise grows and generates employment and profit.

Socially Motivated

The individual must be willing to sign up as a member of PRAYAS as per the memorandum and be willing to donate 10% of all profits to the PRAYAS trust.

Implementation details

Brief intro to our organization

How to identify local businesses:-
 For the development of area around Kharagpur we will find out the following:-

  1. Local businessmen who are interested in working to expand their business and make a social impact
  2. Ideas of businesses that can be started in the area and the interested person who can take it forward.

Process followed:

  1. Our office staff and President are directly connected to the targeted area, so we need to publicize the program through them.
  2. We can conduct a survey of the area. The survey can provide us the following information:
    • The current situation of produced goods in the area
    • The current reach and scale of businesses on the basis of the profits generated by the businesses
    • We can also find the potential people whom we can get hired in the businesses we set up
    • Regarding the idea of new businesses, through the survey, we can get the information on several market sectors and identify the sectors in which the area lacks
  3. We can conduct some workshops regarding our program or about entrepreneurship in the area to identify the interested people to join with the program

Working Structure:-
We will have two pools of members in the program. One will be the members of GYWS and others will be the volunteers and interns of GYWS

Teams of GYWS Members: The recruitment of students under the following teams shall be under :

  1. Fundraising: This team shall be responsible to explore various funding/investment options for the business under PRAYAS. This team will also bring funds for PRAYAS trust from the government or other funding organizations.
  2. Finance: This team will be responsible to handle the finances of the PRAYAS program. The following are the responsibility of the finance team:
    • To manage the payments and keep a record of all the transactions made under PRAYAS
    • To track the expenses made to run the office
    • To get the disbursements done in a particular time period after the approval of the concerned GB member
    • To review the accounts
    • Review the finances of the enterprises and train the entrepreneurs to manage their own finances.
  3. Review and Research: This team will review the proposal submitted by the applicants. In the review process, the team will research for the sources which can be used to make a strategy for the businesses and perform ground research and surveys for validation.
  4. Business Model Development: This team will build a strategy and plan of action for the businesses after their proposal has been reviewed by the review team.
  5. Executive: This team will coordinate with the point of contact in the businesses and also be responsible to monitor that they follow the action plan designed by the business development team.
  6. Marketing: This team will create the content for the Website and all other Social Media Platforms of PRAYAS. They will work on reaching the target audience of PRAYAS through Digital platforms.
